mission

To provide the best engineering solutions and the executable for each of the industry and society.
IDT is working on developing the industry in two frames:
a) Long-term development: By providing students through our programs with experience and knowledge that qualifies them to break into the labor market because they are the engineers and managers of tomorrow.
b) Short-term development: Applying acquired knowledge and providing hands on consultancy instantaneous improvements. IDT is working on the development of both society and industry, where each complements the other.
